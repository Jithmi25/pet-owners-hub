Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on Pet Owners Hub?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right of our homepage. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and enter some basic information about yourself and your pets.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.

How can I update my pet's information?

After logging in, go to your dashboard and select "My Pets." Click on the pet whose information you want to update, make your changes, and be sure to save them.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay for your convenience.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your account settings, selecting "Subscription," and clicking "Cancel Subscription." Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Can I use Pet Owners Hub for multiple pets?

Yes! Our platform allows you to create profiles for all your pets. The basic plan includes up to 3 pets, and our premium plan allows unlimited pets.

How do I contact a veterinarian through the platform?

If you have a premium subscription, you can access our "Ask a Vet" feature 24/7. Go to the "Health" section of your dashboard and select "Consult a Veterinarian" to start a chat or video consultation.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all annual subscriptions. Monthly subscriptions can be canceled at any time, but refunds are not provided for partial months. Please contact our support team for assistance with refund requests.

How can I suggest a new feature?

We love hearing from our users! You can submit feature requests through our "Feedback" section in the app or by emailing suggestions@petownershub.com. Our team reviews all suggestions regularly.
